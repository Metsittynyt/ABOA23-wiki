Quicly about our project

Our main goal is to promote the quality and safety of Finland's waterways, especially around marsh areas. Heavy metals accumulate in slowly forming bogs, from which, especially due to drainage, they are released into the environment along with runoff. Our goal is to research and come up with a solution for how water could be purified from toxic mercury using the cyanobacterium Synechocystis sp. PCC6803 carries.
