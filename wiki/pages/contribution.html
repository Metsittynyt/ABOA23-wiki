The Swamp of Molecular Biology


Our journey with our genes was a rocky one. We decided to go with the blunt ended genes when ordering. There shouldn’t be any reason why they wouldn’t work perfectly fine. Well the assembly failed even after concentrating the genes. So plan B was taken into action and we ordered primers, amplified the genes and added new restriction sites. That failed as well. There was the possibility of blunt ended cloning as well but we decided to go with ordering new genes in plasmids. Every possibility comes with its pros and cons but it’s easy to get lost in the swamp of molecular biology. Our journey cost us time and money but was a good lesson of molecular biology and the DBTL cycle. Hopefully this helps future teams when facing similar problems!
