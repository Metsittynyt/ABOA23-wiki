Introduction


On this page we have gathered all the contributions we’ve made for the iGEM community. This journey has its ups and downs. And those ups and downs have been the most instructive moments during this experience. They have taught us a lot and we feel that by contributing those moments we can help future teams navigate through their project a little easier. Since the project isn’t only about the lab we wanted to make sure to add contributions from other sub teams as well.

Education

As said on our education page, education is a lifelong journey that doesn't end at graduation. We took all age groups into consideration when working on our project’s education. It took a lot of time, work and planning to come up with materials suitable for each particular age group. By giving our materials we hope future teams will make good use of them and all age groups could be included in synthetic biology in the future as well.
